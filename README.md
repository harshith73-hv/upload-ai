## 💡 The Problem

In today's busy world people carry an average of 20–30 unfinished thoughts at any given time — pending deadlines, unresolved worries, things unsaid, family guilt, financial pressure.

When the mind is overwhelmed it **cannot sort itself.**

- Traditional journaling helps but takes **45–60 minutes**
- Googling "how to deal with stress" opens 47 tabs and helps nobody
- Most people skip it entirely and stay mentally stuck
- Result — poor decisions, burnout, and losing emotional connection with the people they love

**The gap:** There was no tool that takes your raw mental chaos and instantly gives you clarity, action, and emotional grounding — all personalised to exactly what YOU wrote.

---

## ✅ The Solution — UnloadAI

UnloadAI is a **mental clarity and emotional recovery companion.**

You type everything in your head with zero filter. The AI reads every single word and returns a fully personalised reset plan in under 20 seconds.

### The 5-Screen Flow

| Screen | What happens |
|---|---|
| **1. Entry** | Mental load slider + brain dump text area |
| **2. Person** | "Who makes everything worth it?" — emotional anchor |
| **3. Results** | 6 personalised AI cards + task breakdown |
| **4. 60s Reset** | Breathing animation → grounding → intention |
| **5. Shift Moment** | Before vs after comparison — your progress made visible |

---

## 🃏 The 6 AI Cards

Every card is generated from **your exact words** — never generic.

1. **What you need to do today** — specific action items extracted from your dump
2. **What you need to let go** — things outside your control, named directly
3. **What is really stressing you** — one honest sentence naming the root cause
4. **What to open right now** — specific apps, searches, and physical actions
5. **For you and [your person]** — emotional message connecting your stress to someone you love
6. **Your next 24 hours** — gentle recovery nudges for tonight and tomorrow

---

## 🔧 How To Actually Do It

Below the 6 cards is a **task breakdown engine.**

For every action item the AI generates:
- The exact tool or app to open
- The exact prompt to type if using AI tools like Claude or ChatGPT
- Step by step instructions
- Realistic time estimate

Example — if your task is *complete hackathon submission:*
> Step 1: Open Lovable and finalise your app — 30 mins
> Step 2: Open Loom and record 5 min demo — 20 mins
> Step 3: Create Google Drive folder and upload files — 10 mins
> Step 4: Fill Google Form and submit — 5 mins
